    About Marrakech Intense

    A heady evocation of its namesake city

    This bold fragrance takes its primary inspirations from the city of Marrakech: the aromas of spices used in its traditional dishes, the intense colours characteristic of the local souks and the surrounding desert, and the warm hospitality integral to Moroccan traditions. It balances spice notes with Jasmine, Neroli and Bergamot to captivating effect.
